## <center>Amber</center>

Amber is a link-saving app that lets you save, organize, and access your URLs across multiple platforms. Think of it as your personal read-later tool — save links from anywhere and retrieve them whenever you need.

Amber is available on three platforms:

- **Amber for Chrome** — a browser extension that lets you save links directly from any page and browse your collection on the new tab page
- **Amber for Obsidian** — a plugin that integrates your saved links inside your Obsidian vault
- **Amber for Mobile** — a mobile app (Flutter) to manage your links on the go

All Amber clients share the same backend: Firebase Auth (Google sign-in) for authentication and Firestore for cloud sync. When offline or not logged in, Amber stores links locally and syncs them automatically on login.
